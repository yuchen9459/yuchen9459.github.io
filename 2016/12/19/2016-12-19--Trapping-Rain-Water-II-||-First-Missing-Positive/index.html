First Missing Positive

Question

Given an unsorted integer array, find the first missing positive integer.

For example,
Given[1,2,0] return 3,
and [3,4,-1,1] return2.

Analysis

LeetCode Discuss

从头到尾遍历，将每个大于0且小于等于长度len的数i都放在数组i-1的位置上，最后再从头遍历一次，找到第一个num[i]!=i+1的即所需结果
